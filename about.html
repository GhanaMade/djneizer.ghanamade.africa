Adrian Emmanuel Neizer Aboagye (born September 24, 1993), popularly known as DJ Neizer, is an award winning Ghanaian disk jockey. He was awarded the Ghana DJ Awards’ Best Campus DJ in May 2017.

Adrian Emmanuel Neizer Aboagye was born on September 24, 1993, in Cape Coast in the Eastern Region of Ghana. He is the eldest of three of siblings.

He graduated from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computational Physics. Before this, he attended the historical Adisadel College, a secondary school in the Eastern Region where he was the entertainment prefect in his last year in the school.
